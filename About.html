OUR HISTORY

Govind Shepley founded TwentyOne06 in 2018 by himself. Without an office or full-time employees, Govind worked out of his apartment - numbered 2106, 6 years later and that passion for design and creativity manifested itself into one of the most established Interior Design firms in the country. We are design challengers. By believing in ourselves and our work, we strive to achieve excellence in the world of design and elevate our clients' experiences.
